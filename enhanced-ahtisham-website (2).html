Premium Coffee Equipment Brand

📈 6.2 ROAS achieved in 11 weeks
Challenge: Milwaukee-based coffee equipment manufacturer was hemorrhaging $18,000 monthly on Amazon PPC. Their flagship $299 espresso machine had only 1.2 ROAS with search terms like "coffee maker" costing $4.50 per click. Organic rank dropped from page 1 to page 3 for "espresso machine" after algorithm update.
Solution: Conducted 3-week keyword audit revealing 67% budget waste on broad match terms. Rebuilt campaigns around 15 high-intent product-specific keywords. Implemented dayparting to avoid 6pm-10pm high-competition window. Added 847 negative keywords including coffee pods, instant coffee, and competitor brands.
Result: ROAS jumped to 6.2, ACoS dropped from 83% to 16%, monthly profit increased by $31,400. Now ranking #2 for "premium espresso machine" and #1 for "stainless steel coffee maker". Customer acquisition cost reduced by 74%.

Organic Skincare Brand

💰 $89K monthly revenue in 7 months
Challenge: California-based organic skincare startup stuck at $22K monthly revenue plateau. Their $47 vitamin C serum was performing well organically but PPC campaigns were unprofitable at 0.9 ROAS. Frequent stockouts during promotional periods caused ranking volatility. Main competitor was outbidding them on 80% of target keywords.
Solution: Shifted strategy from competing on broad "skincare" terms to owning long-tail organic beauty keywords. Launched Sponsored Brand video campaigns showcasing ingredient transparency. Built inventory forecasting model using 18-month sales data to predict demand spikes. Created separate campaigns for different customer segments: anti-aging (35-50), natural beauty (25-35), and sensitive skin (all ages).
Result: Monthly revenue scaled to $89K with maintained 4.1 ROAS. Zero stockouts achieved during Q4 2024 holiday season. Conversion rate improved from 11.2% to 18.7% through listing optimization. Captured 23% market share for "organic vitamin c serum" searches. Customer lifetime value increased 156%.
